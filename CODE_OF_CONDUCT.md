## HDGP Code of Conduct

HDGP is committed to building a safe, respectful, and open collaborative community.  
This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces related to this project, including (but not limited to) repositories, Issues, Pull Requests, discussion forums, document comments, and offline/online events.

---

## 1. Our shared goals

- Build an **open, transparent, auditable, and trustworthy** technical and knowledge ecosystem for HDGP;  
- Support participants from diverse cultures, backgrounds, and disciplines to discuss and improve HDGP together;  
- Maintain a **calm, rational, and non-hostile** atmosphere when discussing sensitive topics such as ethics, values, and risk.

---

## 2. Expected behavior

- **Respect others**  
  - Respect different cultures, positions, experiences, and communication styles;  
  - When you disagree, try to understand concerns first rather than dismissing immediately.

- **Focus on the problem**  
  - Discuss issues based on facts, logic, and technical reasoning where possible;  
  - When criticizing, try to also propose improvements.

- **Be mindful of safety and responsibility**  
  - Avoid publishing detailed technical steps that could be used for abuse or attacks;  
  - If you believe a use case/implementation may cause serious harm, prefer private reporting channels (see `SECURITY.md`).

- **Collaborate and share**  
  - Help newcomers understand the project background and usage;  
  - Contribute FAQs, tutorials, and scenario examples.

---

## 3. Unacceptable behavior

Including (but not limited to):

- Attacks or discriminatory speech based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, political stance, age, disability, etc.;  
- Insults, threats, personal attacks, or sustained harassment;  
- Deliberate misinformation, misrepresentation of others’ views, or malicious distortion of spec clauses;  
- Publicly detailing or encouraging use of HDGP for:
  - Oppression, deprivation, or manipulation of specific individuals or groups;  
  - Mass surveillance, psychological manipulation, addictive design, or other uses contrary to HDGP’s spirit.

---

## 4. Reporting and enforcement

If you experience or witness violations in project spaces, you may:

- When appropriate and safe, politely point out the issue and suggest corrections;  
- If public discussion is not appropriate, report privately through the channels in `README.md` / `SECURITY.md`.

Maintainers may take one or more of the following actions:

- Private communication and warnings;  
- Editing or removing clearly violating content (while preserving necessary auditability);  
- Temporarily or permanently restricting repository participation;  
- In extreme cases, reporting to the relevant platform or institutions.

---

## 5. Scope and evolution

- This Code of Conduct applies to online/offline spaces directly related to this project;  
- If the project is later hosted by a foundation or another entity, the Code of Conduct may be updated and will be announced publicly.

Suggestions via Issues/PRs are welcome.  
By participating in HDGP, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.

## HDGP 社区行为准则（ZH-CN）

HDGP 致力于构建一个安全、尊重、开放的协作社区。  
本行为准则适用于本项目相关的所有空间，包括但不限于：代码仓库、Issue、Pull Request、讨论区、文档评论以及线下/线上会议。

---

## 一、我们的共同目标

- 为“HDGP（厚德归朴）”构建一个**公开透明、可审计、可信赖**的技术与知识生态；  
- 支持来自不同文化、背景与学科的参与者，共同讨论并改进 HDGP；  
- 在涉及伦理、价值观与风险的敏感议题上，维持**冷静、理性、非攻击性**的讨论氛围。

---

## 二、期待的行为

- **尊重他人**  
  - 尊重不同文化、立场、经验与表达方式；  
  - 在不同意见面前，首先尝试理解对方关切，而非立即否定。

- **聚焦问题本身**  
  - 尽量从事实、逻辑与技术角度讨论问题；  
  - 在提出批评时，尽量同时给出改进建议。

- **关注安全与责任**  
  - 在讨论或演示时，避免详细说明可能被用于滥用或攻击的技术细节；  
  - 如认为某个用例/实现可能对个人或群体造成严重伤害，请优先通过更私密的渠道（例如 SECURITY.md 中提供的方式）与维护者沟通。

- **乐于协作与分享**  
  - 鼓励帮助新人理解项目背景与使用方式；  
  - 欢迎主动整理 FAQ、教程或场景示例。

---

## 三、不可接受的行为

包括但不限于：

- 基于种族、性别、性取向、宗教、政治立场、年龄、残障等的攻击或歧视性言论；  
- 侮辱、威胁、人身攻击或长期骚扰行为；  
- 有意传播虚假信息、歪曲他人观点或恶意曲解规范条款；  
- 在公开场合详细描述或鼓励 HDGP 被用于：
  - 压迫、剥夺、操纵特定个人或群体；  
  - 大规模监控、心理操控、成瘾设计等反 HDGP 精神的用途。

---

## 四、报告与处理

如果你在本项目相关空间中遭遇或目击了违反本行为准则的行为，可以：

- 在合适且安全的前提下，礼貌指出问题并建议对方修正；  
- 若不适合公开讨论，可通过仓库维护者在 README / SECURITY 中公布的联系方式进行私下报告。

维护者会根据情况采取以下一种或多种措施：

- 私下沟通与提醒；  
- 编辑或删除明显违规内容（在不影响审计需要的前提下）；  
- 暂时或永久限制相关账号在本仓库的活动权限；  
- 在极端情况下，向所在平台或相关机构举报。

---

## 五、适用范围与演进

- 本行为准则适用于与本项目直接相关的在线/线下空间；  
- 如本项目未来由基金会或其他组织托管，行为准则可能会做相应更新，届时将以公开方式告知。

欢迎通过 Issue / PR 对本行为准则提出改进建议。  
在参与 HDGP 项目的同时，你即默认同意遵守本准则。
